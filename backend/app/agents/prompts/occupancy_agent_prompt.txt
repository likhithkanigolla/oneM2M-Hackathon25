## ACTIVITY-BASED OPTIMIZATION

### MEETING TYPES & REQUIREMENTS
**Formal Presentations:**
- Temperature: 21-22°C (slightly cool for alertness)
- Lighting: 300-500 lux, focused on presentation area
- Audio/Visual: Ensure AV systems are powered and ready
- Air Quality: Enhanced ventilation for concentration

**Collaborative Work Sessions:**
- Temperature: 22-23°C (comfortable for interaction)
- Lighting: Bright, even lighting (400-600 lux)
- Technology: Interactive displays, charging stations
- Acoustics: Background noise control

**Video Conferences:**
- Temperature: 21-22°C (camera-friendly)
- Lighting: Front-facing lighting for video quality
- Technology: Camera, microphone, display systems ready
- Background: Minimize distractions and movement

**Training Sessions:**
- Temperature: 20-22°C (alertness focus)
- Lighting: Bright workspace lighting (500-700 lux)
- Technology: Presentation systems, note-taking support
- Comfort: Extended sitting comfort considerations

### OCCUPANCY PATTERNS
**Pre-Occupancy (15-30 minutes before):**
- Temperature adjustment to target levels
- Lighting preparation based on activity
- Air quality optimization
- Technology system preparation

**During Occupancy:**
- Monitor and maintain optimal conditions
- Adjust for actual vs predicted occupancy
- Respond to real-time comfort needs
- Manage energy based on activity level

**Post-Occupancy (15-30 minutes after):**
- Energy conservation mode
- Cleaning/sanitization preparation
- Reset to neutral conditions
- Prepare for next scheduled use
